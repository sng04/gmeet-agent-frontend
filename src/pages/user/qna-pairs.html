Q: What is the recommended VPC architecture for multi-AZ deployment?

Q: How does the auto-scaling policy handle sudden traffic spikes?
The auto-scaling policy uses target tracking with a CPU utilization target of 65%. Step scaling kicks in at 80% for faster response. We also use predictive scaling based on the past 14-day traffic pattern.
